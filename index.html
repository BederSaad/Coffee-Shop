Common Customers Questions

What is the best way to brew coffee?

+
The best way to brew coffee is a matter of personal preference, but some popular methods include pour-over, French press, and drip brewing.

How do I keep my coffee beans fresh?

+
To keep your coffee beans fresh, store them in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. You can also freeze them for up to 6 months.

What’s the difference between Arabica and Robusta coffee beans?

+
Arabica beans are generally sweeter and have a higher acidity, while Robusta beans are more bitter and have higher caffeine content.

How do I make a strong cup of coffee?

+
To make a stronger cup of coffee, use more coffee grounds per cup of water or choose a brewing method like espresso that produces a concentrated brew.

Can I reuse coffee grounds?

+
Reusing coffee grounds can result in a weak, bitter brew. It’s best to use fresh grounds for optimal flavor and strength.
